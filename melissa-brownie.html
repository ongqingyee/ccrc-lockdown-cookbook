Brownies

Preparation time: 10 mins; Cooking time 60 mins

Submitted by: Melissa Hart

Background

Brownies are my favourite American food. However in Australia we don't tend to do them so well- too dry,too much like cake. Until I found this Donna Hay recipe! These are crispy on the outside and gooey on the inside. Yum!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (150g) plain flour
  • ¾ cup (75g) cocoa
  • ¾ cup (130g) brown sugar
  • 1⅓ cups (290g) caster sugar
  • 175g unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (sometimes I forget this bit)
  • 3 eggs 125g dark chocolate, chopped (I like to use Lindt dark chocolate with sea salt)

Instructions

    Place the flour, cocoa, both the sugars, the butter, vanilla and eggs in a large bowl and mix until smooth. Add the chocolate and stir to combine. Preheat oven to 160°C . Lightly grease a 20cm square tin and line with non-stick baking paper. Spread the mixture into the tin. Bake for 50 minutes – 1 hour or until the brownie is set+. Allow to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before slicing into squares and serving warm, or allow to cool completely in the tin.
