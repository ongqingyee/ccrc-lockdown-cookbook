Elonaâ€™s Mexican Bean Mix For Nachos Or Tacos

Preparation time: 10 mins; Cooking time 30 mins

Submitted by: Elona Rey-Costa

Background

It's just bloody delicious and if I didn't live with my family, I would be eating this every day.

Ingredients

  • 1 brown/white onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 zucchini
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 red capsicum
  • 1 yellow/green capsicum (the more colours the better)
  • 1 can of 4 beans mix
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 can diced tomato
  • 1 large jar of salsa
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chilli (to your taste)

Instructions

  • Chop all the veggies, onion & garlic (I like to chop them into small cubes)
  • Rinse your beans.
  • Throw the onions and garlic into the pan on medium heat with some oil and let them brown for a couple of minutes.
  • Once the onions are browned, throw in all your chopped veggies. Let them heat up in the pan until they are cooked through and become soft. This will take between 5-10 mins
  • Once the veggies are cooked, throw your beans into the pan and stir. Let them cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Add your can of diced tomato & jar of salsa and stir.
  • Season with all the spices to taste. I'm a big fan of paprika so I like to add lots of that in. Halve the amount of cumin & oregano compared to paprika and top it with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Let this simmer on the stove for another 5 minutes.
  • Serve with guacamole, sour cream, cheese & corn chips OR throw the mixture into some wraps/taco shells. Whatever you fancy.
